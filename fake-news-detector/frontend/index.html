About DeFakeAI

Born from the urgent need to combat misinformation in our digital age, DeFakeAI represents a breakthrough in automated fact-checking technology. Our platform combines advanced machine learning, natural language processing, and a vast database of verified information to provide real-time fact verification.

Our Mission

We're dedicated to empowering users with the tools they need to identify and verify information accurately. Our mission extends beyond simple fact-checking – we aim to promote digital literacy and critical thinking in an era where misinformation can spread rapidly across social media and news platforms.

Our Technology

DeFakeAI leverages cutting-edge BERT-based natural language processing models, trained on millions of verified news articles and fact-checks. Our system analyzes content across multiple dimensions including linguistic patterns, source credibility, temporal consistency, and cross-references with our extensive database of verified information.

Our Impact

Since our launch, we've helped verify over 10 million news statements and claims, supporting journalists, researchers, and everyday users in their quest for truth. Our API is used by leading news organizations and educational institutions worldwide, making fact-checking accessible and efficient.
